Part Analyzer Privacy Policy

Part Analyzer is offered as conventional desktop application which is deemed useful without the need personal information. Part Analyzer does not collect and transit any personal information. Its purposes is to provide user information about their 3D models without the need to transmit that information outside of the user's computer.

In the current beta release, diagnostic data is stored and transmit to AppCenter.ms, but this information only includes internal behavior of the program. No user information of input data files provided to Medema Part Analyzer are transmitted.

If you have any concerns about this privacy statement, please contact Medema Labs at contract@medemalabs.com.
